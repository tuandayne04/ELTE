Characters

  • Takaki Tono:
    The protagonist of the film. He is a kind and introspective boy who is deeply in love with Akari
  • Akari Shinohara:
    Takaki's best friend and love interest. She is a warm and caring girl who is also struggling to come to terms with her feelings for Takaki.
  • Kanae Sumida:
    Takaki's classmate in high school. She is a kind and supportive girl who has a crush on Takaki

Themes

  • Love: The film explores the different aspects of love, including first love, long-distance relationships, and lost love.
  • Loss: The film also deals with the theme of loss, both in the context of romantic relationships and in the broader sense of life.
  • Passage of time: The film shows how the passage of time can change people and relationships.

Critical reception

5 Centimeters per Second was a critical and commercial success, grossing over $30 million worldwide. The film was praised for its beautiful animation, emotional resonance, and realistic portrayal of love and loss.

However, some critics found the film to be too slow and melancholic. They also criticized the lack of closure at the end of the film.

Overall, 5 Centimeters per Second is a well-made and moving film that explores the themes of love, loss, and the passage of time in a realistic and relatable way. It is a film that will stay with you long after you have seen it.
